FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Where does the Generate Statement button appear?
It appears in the top button bar of the customer form view inside the Invoicing app. To reach it: go to Invoicing → Customers, remove the default "Customer Invoices" filter from the search bar, then open any customer record. The button will be visible at the top of the form.
Does it work without creating a new invoice first?
Yes. The module reads existing invoices and payments from your database. As long as a customer has posted invoices, you can generate a statement straight away — no need to create any new records.
What does "Date Range" filter on?
The date range filters on Invoice Date for invoices, and on the payment registration date for payments. Make sure your invoices are dated within the selected range for them to appear in the statement.
Which Odoo apps does this module require?
Only the Invoicing (account) app. No Sales, CRM, or any other app is required.
What are the three filter options?
  • All Invoices & Payments — full account history in the date range
  • Unpaid / Partially Paid Only — open and partially settled invoices and their payments
  • Paid Invoices Only — fully settled invoices and their payments
Will it show my company's logo on the statement?
The statement uses a clean, branded layout that pulls your company name and company email from your Odoo company settings. To display your logo, add it under Settings → General Settings → Companies.
Does it work with multiple companies?
Yes. The statement always uses the company linked to the current user session, so it is safe to use in multi-company Odoo setups without any extra configuration.
Can I send the statement by email directly from the wizard?
The current version generates the statement as a downloadable PDF only. Once downloaded, you can attach it to any email from your mail client. An email-sending option may be added in a future update.
Is this compatible with Odoo 16.0 Community and Enterprise?
Yes. The module is built and tested on Odoo 16.0 and works on both Community and Enterprise editions.
How do I get support?
Reach out via the support email listed on the module page. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.
